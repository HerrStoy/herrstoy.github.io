Carbonara

Description

Carbonara is delicious and eggy. The bacon makes this really worth it and makes the carbonara special compared to other spaghetti dishes.

A shitty carbonara is usually a great spaghetti, but don't get the one in Toscana in Lillehammer, as it might make you shit your guts out the following night.

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Egg
  • Bacon
  • Love

Steps

  1. Boil pasta
  2. At the same time fry bacon
  3. Add pasta to frying pan and add egg
  4. Serve with parmesan cheese
